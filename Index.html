My Wish List is a revolutionary app that uses cutting edge technology to help you plan a wedding, baby shower, or to just create a Christmas or birthday list.

My Wish List is the perfect app to help you organize and manage items you Wish you had. No longer are you restricted by retailers who allow you to add items to only their lists.

By allowing you to scan items directly to your iPhone from any store My Wish List finally brings the ease and convenience of updating and sharing wish lists to you.

My Wish List was designed with you the consumer in mind. Unlike other apps, My Wish List relies on you to grant a friends wish, rather than relying on retailers to remove items off lists when purchased which is often unreliable.

  • Scan any item with a UPC using your iPhone
  • Update your Wish Lists in real time and send Wish Lists to your friends when you add and delete items on your Wish Lists
  • Pushes notifications and badges notify you when your friends have updated their Wish Lists
  • Invite friends to use My Wish List via Text Message or Email
  • Uses Google maps to locate retailers' closet to you for items on your Wish List or a friends Wish List
  • Manage, categorize, and personalize your Wish Lists
  • Allows your friends to "Grant Your Wish"
  • Displays online pricing for items on your Wish List or a friend's Wish List
